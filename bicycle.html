A history of innovation

Sakai, Osaka Prefecture

Bicycle components

Shimano, Inc., is known around the world for fishing tackle and rowing equipment, but its flagship product has always been bicycle components. Shimano Shozaburo, the son of a Sakai metalworker, established Shimano Iron Works in 1921. His only machine was a borrowed lathe. Just 15 years later, the company offered this bicycle to the Imperial Household as a gift for the Crown Prince, who would later become Akihito, the Heisei Emperor.
